Steve Jobs

Founder of apple

"Stay Hungry, stay foolish"

Here's a time line of Steve Jobs' life

  • February 24, 1955 - Born in san francisco and adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs
  • February 24, 1972 - Graduates Homestead High School
  • February 24, 1976 - Co-founds Apple Computer
  • September 12, 1985 - Steve Jobs resigns from apple
  • Febuary 3, 1986 - Steve Jobs creates Pixar Animation Studios
  • November 25, 1995 - Becomes CEO of Pixar
  • December 10, 1996 - Returns to Apple
  • August 1, 2004 - Goes through surgery to remove a cancerous tumor in his pancreas
  • August 24, 2011 - Resigns as CEO from apple, becomes chairman
  • October 5, 2011 - Passes away from pancreatic cancer
